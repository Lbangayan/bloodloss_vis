Affects of Anesthesia on Blood Loss

Perhaps the greatest fear when it comes to going under the table is the blood loss you will undergo. Blood loss is no serious matter; excessive blood loss during a surgery can lead to postoperative complications like organ failure, needing blood transfusions, or sadly, even death. Specifically, a lot of patients wonder about what drug they take prior to the operation and how it relates to the amount of blood that is lost during the surgery. For those who have done surgery before, you have probably heard the doctor advising you not to take medication like coagulants to reduce blood loss prior to surgery. Certain drugs can aid with or have no effect on blood loss, while others can prove detrimental.
